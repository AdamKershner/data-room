# 03 — Market and Customers

[← Back to index](./README.md) · Prev: [02 — Product today](./02-product-today.md) · Next: [04 — Revenue model](./04-revenue-model.md)

## Executive summary

Kahana serves **creators who sell curated digital products** and **buyers who want discovery plus trust**. **GTM priority:** women experts on the supply side and women audiences on the demand side.

---

## GTM priority: women-first

| Side | Focus |
|------|-------|
| **Supply** | Women experts, coaches, educators, influencers packaging digital knowledge |
| **Demand** | Women audiences seeking trusted, curated expertise |

Discovery motion: find → filter → invite → amplify on Explore (priority categories: beauty, fashion, health-wellness, lifestyle).

## Buyer personas (sketch)

### "Curious buyer"

- Discovers via Explore search or social link
- Compares 2–3 hubs before purchase
- Price-sensitive; needs clear value prop on hub page

### "Creator superfan"

- Follows specific creator from social
- Lands on profile → buys multiple hubs
- High LTV; low price sensitivity
