# 05 — Growth Strategy

[← Back to index](./README.md) · Prev: [04 — Revenue model](./04-revenue-model.md) · Next: [06 — Unit economics](./06-unit-economics.md)

## Executive summary

Kahana grows through a **marketplace flywheel** with **women-first expert curation** as the primary GTM engine: discover, filter, invite, and amplify high-quality women creators.

---

## Primary GTM: women-first expert curation

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| **Discover** | Find women experts, coaches, educators, influencers |
| **Filter** | Assess catalog quality, audience fit, expertise depth |
| **Invite** | Partner and showcase hubs on Explore |
| **Amplify** | Featured creators, category campaigns |

**Not yet:** Paid ads at scale; enterprise outbound. Creator-led + curated partnerships first.

**Traction:** 6,500+ registered users; network effects compound as catalog quality grows.

---

## Growth flywheel

```mermaid
flowchart LR
  Creators[More creators]
  Catalog[Richer catalog]
  Buyers[More buyers]
  GMV[Higher GMV]
  Earnings[Creator earnings]
  Creators --> Catalog
  Catalog --> Buyers
  Buyers --> GMV
  GMV --> Earnings
  Earnings --> Creators
```

**Break the flywheel anywhere and growth stalls.** Priority order when stuck:

1. No catalog → recruit creators and reduce publish friction
2. No traffic → distribution and SEO
3. No conversion → paywall, trust, hub page quality
4. No retention → creator success and product value

**Trigger moments for upgrade prompt:**

- Attempt to create 4th hub
- Upload limit hit on hub
