## Executive summary

Kahana combines a public marketplace (Explore), creator storefronts (profiles + hubs), Stripe monetization, and collaboration tooling at **app.kahana.io**. Operationally we are the **Amazon of digital products**; visionally we aspire to be **Wan Shi Tong's Library** for humanity.

## Vision: The Library

Wan Shi Tong's Library is a vast, ancient repository — books, scrolls, artifacts covering all human knowledge. Kahana expresses this as **digital artifacts** (guides, playbooks, templates, resource libraries) curated from top experts, creators, and influencers, accessible worldwide.

| Library parallel | Kahana expression |
|------------------|-------------------|
| Vast repository | Explore marketplace + hubs across 16 categories |
| Knowledge Seekers | Creator partners + discovery filters |
| Exchange of knowledge | Creators publish; buyers access free and paid hubs |
| Mesmerizing depth | Explore UX that rewards curiosity |

**Commitments:** Substantial free quality content for humanity. Curate for empowerment. Women experts and women audiences first in GTM.

## What "Amazon of digital products" means (operational frame)

| Dimension | Amazon parallel | Kahana expression |
|-----------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Selection** | Huge catalog | Explore with 16 categories, hub + creator discovery |
| **Trust** | Reviews, known sellers | Creator profiles, pricing clarity, Stripe checkout |
| **Convenience** | One account, fast checkout | Browse → buy → access; Linktree-style profiles |
| **Scale** | Marketplace economics | 5% take rate + Growth SaaS ($9.99/mo) |

## Positioning statement

**For creators** who package expertise into digital products, **Kahana** is a marketplace and storefront for curated hubs, Explore discovery, and Stripe monetization.

**For buyers**, Kahana is a curated digital marketplace for high-signal products — unlike scattered PDFs and one-off Gumroad links.

---

## What Kahana is

- A **marketplace** (Explore at `/explore`)
- A **creator storefront** (profiles + monetized hubs)
- A **collaboration layer** (hubs with files, notes, unlimited collaborators on Free)
- A **SaaS platform** (Free / Growth / Enterprise)

**Product fact:** Brand is Kahana; legal entity is Kahana Group Inc. Site: https://app.kahana.io
