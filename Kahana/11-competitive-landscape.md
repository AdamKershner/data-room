# 11 — Competitive Landscape

[← Back to index](./README.md) · Prev: [10 — Technical roadmap](./10-technical-roadmap.md)

## Executive summary

Kahana competes in the **creator economy** (sell digital products, memberships, courses) and aspires to **Wan Shi Tong–style discovery** — curious people wandering a trusted library of expert knowledge, not anonymous text-only encyclopedias.

**Full interactive map:** data room page at `/kahana-competitors`

**Oasis Browser competitors** (enterprise/consumer browsers) remain separate at `/competitors` (archive).

---

## Comparison dimensions

| Dimension | What it measures |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Discovery** | Public marketplace browse vs link-in-bio / owned audience only |
| **Storefront** | Branded hub + checkout vs external checkout or platform-native only |
| **Knowledge depth** | Multi-format curated libraries (PDF, video, files, text) vs single-format |
| **Trust** | Verified experts, commerce trust signals vs anonymous or low-trust UGC |

---

## Kahana positioning

| Dimension | Kahana |
|-----------|--------|
| Discovery | **Strong** — Explore marketplace at app.kahana.io/explore |
| Storefront | **Strong** — Profiles + monetized hubs + Stripe Connect |
| Knowledge depth | **Strong** — Multi-format resource libraries in hubs |
| Trust | **Strong** — Verified creators, commerce signals, curated catalog |

**Customer stack Kahana replaces:** Gumroad or Stan (sales) + Notion/Drive (delivery) + Linktree (bio links) + social for audience → **one platform**.
