Document Title: Risk Treatment Plan – Kahana Oasis
Version: 1.0
Effective Date: March 2026
Owner: Security Lead / Compliance Lead
Approved By: CTO / CEO (Adam Kershner)

1. Purpose
This Risk Treatment Plan defines how Kahana addresses risks identified in the Information Security Risk Register and Risk Assessment Report. It assigns treatment decisions, owners, actions, and status so remediation can be tracked and demonstrated for SOC 2.

2. Scope
This plan applies to all in-scope risks affecting Kahana Oasis and supporting systems (AWS, Supabase, CI/CD, logging/monitoring, vendor dependencies, and related security/compliance operations).

3. Treatment Options
- Mitigate: reduce likelihood and/or impact with additional controls
- Accept: formally accept residual risk with documented rationale and review date
- Transfer: shift part of risk via contractual/insurance/vendor obligations
- Avoid: discontinue the risky activity/process

5. Governance and Review
- This plan is reviewed at least quarterly for status updates and blockers.
- High risks and accepted risks are reviewed with leadership.
- Any accepted risk must include documented rationale, compensating controls, and next review date.
- Changes to risk status must be synchronized with:
  - Information-Security-Risk-Register
  - Risk-Assessment-Report

6. Evidence and Audit Readiness
Kahana retains treatment evidence (tickets, approvals, screenshots/exports, logs, test results) in designated evidence locations to support SOC 2 readiness and audit sampling.
