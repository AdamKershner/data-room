Document Title: Information Security Risk Register – Kahana Oasis
Version: 1.0
Effective Date: March 2026
Owner: Security Lead / Compliance Lead
Approved By: CTO / CEO

1. Purpose
This register tracks Kahana's identified information security and operational risks for Kahana Oasis in support of SOC 2 risk management expectations. It is the operational tracking artifact paired with:
- Risk Assessment Report
- Risk Treatment Plan

2. Status Legend
- Open: Risk identified; treatment not started or incomplete
- In Progress: Mitigation work underway
- Mitigated: Planned treatment complete; residual risk accepted as manageable
- Accepted: Risk formally accepted by leadership with rationale

4. Operating Rules
- Every risk in this register must have a corresponding treatment entry in the Risk Treatment Plan.
- Status and owner must be updated whenever mitigation progress changes.
- High risks must be reviewed with leadership and are not accepted without explicit approval.
- Register is reviewed at least quarterly; full risk assessment is reviewed at least annually and after major incidents/changes.
