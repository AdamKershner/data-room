Document Title: Organization Chart – Kahana Oasis (SOC 2)
Version: 1.0
Effective Date: March 2026
Owner: CEO / Security Lead

1. Purpose
This document defines Kahana's reporting structure and key control ownership roles for SOC 2 scope. It clarifies who is accountable for security, engineering, operations, compliance, and incident-related decisions.

2. Current Reporting Structure (Kahana-specific)

Board / Founders
  -> Chief Executive Officer (CEO): Adam Kershner
      -> Chief Technology Officer (CTO): [Name]
          -> Engineering (application development, SDLC, code review)
          -> DevOps / Infrastructure (AWS, Supabase operations, CI/CD, backup/DR)
      -> Security Lead / CISO: [Name]
          -> Security & Compliance activities (risk, policy, SOC 2 controls, incident response)
      -> Operations / Business Functions
          -> Customer Support / Success
          -> HR / People Operations
          -> Legal / Privacy support (internal or external counsel)

3. Executive Roles and Accountability

CEO – Adam Kershner
- Ultimate executive accountability for Kahana Oasis, SOC program sponsorship, and major policy approvals.
- Reviews material risk, incident communications, and major remediation decisions.

CTO – [Name]
- Owns technology strategy and architecture for Oasis.
- Accountable for secure engineering, deployment controls, and technical reliability.
- Oversees Engineering and DevOps / Infrastructure.

Security Lead / CISO – [Name]
- Owns information security program and SOC 2 control design/operation.
- Leads risk assessments, policy lifecycle, and incident response coordination.
- Coordinates control testing/evidence readiness with Engineering, Ops, and leadership.

4. Functional Reporting Detail

Engineering (reports to CTO)
- Implements product features and fixes through SDLC, code review, and change management controls.
- Supports security remediation and incident follow-up actions.

DevOps / Infrastructure (reports to CTO)
- Operates AWS us-east-1 and Supabase production environment.
- Maintains CI/CD, infrastructure-as-code, backup/restore, and monitoring controls.
- Executes technical recovery procedures during continuity/DR events.

Security & Compliance (reports to Security Lead / CISO)
- Maintains SOC 2 policy framework and control mapping.
- Runs risk management process (assessment, register, treatment tracking).
- Coordinates security monitoring, incident handling, and post-incident review actions.

Customer Support / Success (reports via Operations to CEO)
- Manages customer-facing communications, including during incidents and continuity events.
- Handles customer requests under data handling and privacy policies.

HR / People Operations (reports via Operations to CEO)
- Runs onboarding/offboarding and security awareness requirements.
- Ensures workforce acknowledgment of security and acceptable use requirements.

Legal / Privacy (reports via Operations/Leadership)
- Reviews contracts, DPAs, and breach/notification obligations.
- Advises on data handling and retention obligations.

5. SOC 2 Alignment Notes
- Reporting lines support clear accountability for SOC 2 control operation and oversight.
- CTO and Security Lead ownership is separated for technical control execution vs. security governance.
- CEO provides top-level governance and escalation authority for material incidents and risk decisions.

6. Maintenance
This organization chart is reviewed at least annually, and upon significant organizational changes (new executives, role changes, or control ownership changes). Updates must be approved by CEO and Security Lead.
