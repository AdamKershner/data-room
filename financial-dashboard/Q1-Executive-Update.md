## Sales & enterprise pipeline

- **Inbound enterprise prospect:** An **HR technology company (500+ employees)** reached out through **organic channels** for **pricing** and to **evaluate Oasis in a vendor selection**. Indicative interest on the order of **~$25K annually** for an enterprise browser deployment.
- **Expanding prospecting** with a repeatable process to identify on the order of **~1,000 new prospects per week** — heads of IT, security teams, enterprise solutions architects, and CISOs.
- Early LinkedIn outbound: **over 10%** of connection requests to this ICP are **accepted**, so the reachable buyer audience is **growing week over week**.
- **Q2 goal:** at least **three signed letters of intent (LOIs)** for pilots.
- **Pilot pricing:** **$12,000 per year per company** — flat fee to deploy the Oasis enterprise browser for the pilot (not seat-based).

- Conversations with **connectors** and prospective **pre-seed and seed** investors **now**, so relationships are warm ahead of formal diligence.
