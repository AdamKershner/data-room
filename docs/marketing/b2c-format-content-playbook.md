## Format specs (use consistently)

| Format | Spec |
|--------|------|
| **LinkedIn carousel** | 5‚Äì8 slides, 1080√ó1080 or PDF carousel; one idea per slide; engagement question or soft CTA on last slide |
| **Short-form video** | 15‚Äì45s; Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts; vertical 9:16 primary; text-on-screen OK; VO optional |
| **Humble animation** | Stick figures, hand-drawn icons, whiteboard/notebook look; wobbly lines; concepts over polish, see style guide below |
| **LinkedIn poll** | 2‚Äì4 options; 1-week run; use results to pick the follow-up post |
| **Blog post** | Kahana blog; 800‚Äì1,500 words; footnoted stats; SEO-friendly H2s |

## Voice and legal guardrails

| Stage | Rules |
|-------|--------|
| **Name the change** | Ad Nauseam voice; no Oasis; no Product Hunt hook |
| **Name the villain** | Structural proof; sourced stats; no Oasis product pitch |
| **Winners and losers** | Futures, not blame; bridge OK; full PH hook only on launch assets (`mn-ph-chrome-02`+) |
| **Promised land (REFUGE)** | Imagine / tease; codename REFUGE OK; **no Oasis name** in public tease posts |
| **Magic gifts** | Oasis can be named; one gift per asset; honest partial claims (ETP, etc.) |
| **Evidence** | Poem, proof, launch CTAs; PH hook on launch-day assets |

**Legal:** Do not claim ‚Äúzero tracking‚Äù or ‚Äúwe don‚Äôt collect data‚Äù unless comms/legal approves. Comparative Chrome lines need review in gifts and evidence stages.

## Humble animation style guide

**Aesthetic:** Looks like a smart kid explained a hard idea on a napkin, not a SaaS explainer from a studio.

**Do:**

- Black or dark pen on off-white; optional single accent color (teal or orange)
- Stick figures with circle heads and line limbs; wobble paths slightly frame-to-frame
- Handwritten labels (or a casual script font that reads handwritten)
- Simple icons: mosquitoes = dots, ads = rectangles, browser = rounded rectangle, attention = coins or battery draining
- Gentle humor; pause on the concept frame for 2‚Äì3 seconds
- Export 1080√ó1920 for Reels/Shorts; 1080√ó1080 square cut optional

**Don‚Äôt:**

- 3D renders, lens flares, corporate gradients, stock ‚Äúdiverse team at laptop‚Äù footage
- Over-polished motion graphics or brand guideline perfection
- More than one new metaphor per 20-second clip

**Example motifs to reuse across sections:**

1. **Trap tab**: tab pulls a lever; coins fly out labeled ‚Äúattention‚Äù
2. **Mosquito cloud**: dots orbit a stick figure at a laptop
3. **Pipeline crayon**: scroll ‚Üí profile box ‚Üí ‚Äúauction‚Äù gavel
4. **Two paths**: fork in road; ‚Äúact‚Äù vs ‚Äúwait‚Äù
5. **Refuge room**: storm of pop-ups outside; calm box inside
6. **Boulder + wrench**: one obstacle removed per gift

**Tools (suggested):** Procreate + simple export, Excalidraw animation, After Effects with roughen edges, or Remotion with hand-drawn SVGs, pick what ships fast.
