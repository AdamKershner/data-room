## Product-backed promises (REFUGE voice) {#product-backed-promises}

Synthesized from [oasis-your-data-and-training.md](marketing/oasis-your-data-and-training.md). **Tease posts use REFUGE / “imagine”, not Oasis.**

### Pillar 1: Browsing stays on your machine

- What you read, bookmark, and open stays on **your machine** for everyday browsing and for assistant features that search tabs, groups, and history.
- Your history helps **you** inside the browser on your computer; it is not packaged and sold as a browsing profile.
- **REFUGE promise (tease):** *Imagine a refuge where memory and search serve your work on your device, not an ad graph in the cloud.*

**Task:** `mn-promised-chrome-06`

### Pillar 2: Assistant on your terms

- Product improvement data is tied to **how you use the assistant**, not a wholesale export of everything you browse.
- **Personalize with my account** is **off** by default; interaction logs are not tied to your identity unless you opt in.
- **Anonymous** means **not linked to your account**, not “nothing leaves your device.” Using the assistant still uses network AI to generate answers.
- **REFUGE promise (tease):** *Imagine sidebar help that gets sharper without defaulting to a linked pipeline you never chose.*

**Task:** `mn-promised-chrome-07`

### Training (launch / in-product: not Promised Land tease)

Thumbs up/down training with optional **+1,000 bonus AI tokens** for qualifying submissions (signed in, category, badge, 30+ char comment). See source doc Training: use in Present evidence evidence or product comms, not pre-launch REFUGE tease.

## Publishable tease drafts (no Oasis name)

### Draft A: Local refuge (`mn-promised-chrome-06`)

Imagine a browser where your tabs, bookmarks, and history stay **on your machine**, searchable when you need them, not uploaded as a profile for advertisers to mine. Imagine fewer cross-site tracker bites because the refuge is built on a privacy-first engine, not a promise of zero ads, but a promise that **your browsing is not the product**. We are building toward that vision. We call it **REFUGE**.

### Draft B: Assistant on your terms (`mn-promised-chrome-07`)

Imagine AI in the sidebar that helps you group tabs, close duplicates, and get unstuck, while **personalization stays off by default**. Improvement logs are not tied to your identity unless you turn that on. That is not “zero data”; it is **no account linkage by default**, honest boundaries, not magic. **REFUGE** is the name we use while we build.

### Draft C: Combined (optional thread closer)

Ad Nauseam is the feeling; Chrome is the environment. **REFUGE** is what we are building toward: your history on your machine, help on your terms. Proof comes next, one gift at a time.

## Guardrails (product-backed)

| Do not say (tease or launch without legal) | Say instead |
|--------------------------------------------|-------------|
| “Zero data” / “we collect nothing” | Anonymous by default = **no account linkage** on assistant improvement logs |
| “Zero tracking” on the entire web | Firefox ETP = **fewer** cross-site bites; ads may still appear |
| Oasis blocks Gemini-in-Chrome | Oasis governs **Oasis assistant** telemetry; does not disable third-party browser AI |
| Sell browsing history | History helps **you** on device; not sold to advertisers |
