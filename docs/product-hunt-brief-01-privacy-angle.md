# Oasis Browser: Product Hunt Launch

## BRIEF 01: THE PRIVACY ANGLE

**"Your browser works for Google. Ours works for you."**

Direct contrast positioning: us vs. them.

---

## THE HOOK

Your browser works for Google. Ours works for you.

Simple. Binary. No room for misunderstanding. Every person who has ever noticed an ad follow them across the internet will feel this.

---

## AUDIENCE

Tech-savvy individuals who are privacy-aware but haven't switched browsers because nothing has felt worth switching for. Also, IT/security professionals who evaluate tools for their teams. Both groups respond to direct, no-nonsense language.

---

## THE CORE INSIGHT

People know Chrome works for Google and is built for advertisers. They've accepted it as a fact of modern life. We refuse to let them accept it and position Oasis as the first real alternative that gives control back to the user.

---

## EMOTION TO TRIGGER

Mild outrage + recognition first: *"wait, yeah, that is what's happening."* Then relief: *"finally, someone built the thing that fixes this."* The tone is empowering, not scary.

**Narrative spine (checklist):** [Ad Nauseam villain](marketing-narrative-checklist) → [recognition bridge](marketing-narrative-checklist) (`mn-ph-chrome-01`) → this hook → Product Hunt assets (May 27, 2026).

---

## VISUAL CONCEPT: SOCIAL

Split screen. Left side: Chrome logo surrounded by data collection icons (ad targets, location pins, search history). Labeled **"Working for Google."** Right side: Oasis logo, clean, calm, empty of trackers. Labeled **"Working for you."** No clutter. Let the contrast do the work. Use brand colors for the Oasis side.

---

## VISUAL CONCEPT: PRODUCT HUNT HEADER

Full-width banner. Left half dark/muted (Chrome's world: noise, data, surveillance). Right half clean and bright (Oasis: calm, private, yours). The Oasis circular logo centered on the right half. Tagline below the logo: **"Your browser works for Google. Ours works for you."** Use brand font and size specs.

---

## VISUAL CONCEPT: VIDEO (15–30 SEC)

1. Open on someone searching for something personal: a health symptom, a salary, a relationship question.
2. Cut to: ads immediately following them. Same topic. Everywhere. Email. Social. News.
3. Text on screen: **"Your browser just watched that."**
4. Cut to: same person, same searches, in Oasis. Nothing follows them. Clean feed.
5. Text on screen: **"Your browser works for Google. Ours works for you."**
6. End card: Oasis logo + **"Now on Product Hunt"** + URL.

No voiceover required: on-screen text only. Subtitles if audio used.

---

## COPY DIRECTION: PRODUCT HUNT DESCRIPTION

Lead with the problem:

> Every search you do in Chrome is a data point. Every click. Every diagnosis you looked up. Every salary you researched. Chrome collects it. Google profits from it. We built Oasis because your browser should work for you and not for an ad network.

Keep it under **150 words**.

End with the CTA: **"Try it free. Own your data."**

---

## COPY DIRECTION: SOCIAL CAPTION

Short:

> You searched for it once. It followed you for a week. That is not a coincidence. That is your browser working for Google. Oasis works for you. Link in bio.

Pair with the split-screen visual.

## COMMS GUARDRAILS

- Do not claim "zero tracking" or absolute privacy unless legal approves exact wording.
- Contrast Chrome's **linked** data model with Oasis using approved product language.
- Attribute third-party stats (Surfshark, Forbes, etc.) when used in supporting posts, not required in the hook line itself.
