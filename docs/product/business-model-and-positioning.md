# Business model and positioning (marketing)

**Version:** Oasis `v1.0.0.12`  
**Purpose:** Approved narrative phrases for Magic gifts magic gifts and comparative copy. Not a substitute for legal review on external ads.

---

## Approved positioning (Checklist · Magic gifts intro)

| Phrase | Meaning for copy |
|--------|------------------|
| **Not built on an ads business** | Oasis does not monetize by auctioning user attention to advertisers the way the default browser colossus does (see Name the villain villain narrative). |
| **Free forever** | No paywall on the browser for core use; business succeeds when the product is genuinely useful. |
| **Performance as product** | Revenue and retention tie to productivity outcomes (faster work, fewer interruptions), not impression volume. |

**Pair with:** [b2c-chrome-villain-copy.md](../b2c-chrome-villain-copy.md) for Chrome/ad-stack evidence; do not re-litigate the full villain essay in every Magic gifts post.

## Do-not-claim (positioning layer)

- Do **not** say “zero ads,” “off the grid,” or “we collect nothing” without qualifiers ([privacy-data-and-telemetry.md](privacy-data-and-telemetry.md)).
- Do **not** imply Oasis disables **Gemini-in-Chrome** or third-party browser telemetry.
- Do **not** promise “free forever” in fine print that contradicts future paid tiers without updating this doc.
